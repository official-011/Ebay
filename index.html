About This Site

eBay is a global marketplace for buying and selling goods. This site provides beginner-friendly guides, tips, and simulated tools to help you get started. Remember, always follow eBay's policies for safe transactions.

Selling on eBay

Steps to sell: 1. Create an eBay account. 2. List your item with photos and description. 3. Set a price and shipping. 4. Monitor bids and ship promptly.

Tips: Use high-quality photos, accurate descriptions, and competitive pricing. Avoid scams by verifying buyers.

Purchasing on eBay

Steps to buy: 1. Search for items. 2. Check seller ratings and reviews. 3. Place a bid or buy it now. 4. Pay securely and wait for delivery.

Tips: Use eBay's filters for condition and location. Read return policies. Protect yourself with PayPal.
